About EXPLICIT

Our Legacy

Founded in 2006 as SPECS (Students Proficient in Electronics and Communication System), we transitioned to EXPLICIT in 2010, marking a new era of technological exploration.

Our Logo

The EXPLICIT logo embodies our identity through symbols representing the BSIT program's founding year, logic gates, programming languages, and computer hardware.

Beyond Academics

We host annual events like General Assembly and IT Week, participate in school initiatives, and maintain computer labs through hands-on member activities.
